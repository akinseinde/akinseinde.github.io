Hi! I'm Samuel I. Akinseinde, a professor of Technical Education.
I earned my Master’s Degree from Michigan State University, East Lansing, USA, and PhD from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka with both degrees in Industrial Technical Education and specialization in Electrical/Electronics. My current research interests include; Gender issue, Policies, Leadership, Programme evaluation, Vocational – Technical Education and Training.
